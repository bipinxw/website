Overview

RoleRadar.ai is an innovative job-matching platform designed to help users find the perfect job opportunities based on their professional experience. The platform leverages AI to curate personalized job listings by analyzing job descriptions and matching them to a user's resume.

Key Features

  • AI Job Matching: Matches user resumes with job listings based on semantic similarity using a BERT-based model.
  • Easy Search: Enter your job designation and fetch curated job listings.
  • Resume Upload & Processing: Upload your resume and get scores for each job based on how well they match your experience.
  • Real-Time Results: Receive personalized job opportunities with detailed insights.
  • Customizable User Flow: The application offers a simple and streamlined interface for an intuitive user experience.

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3 & Javascript for minimalistic, responsive UI with animations.
  • Backend: Flask for API requests and job processing.
  • AI: BERT and Cosine Similarity for advanced semantic matching.
  • Scraping: SerpApi for fetching job listings.

How It Works

The platform matches jobs to your resume through a simple 5-step process:

  1. Enter your job designation (e.g., "Product Manager").
  2. The system fetches relevant job listings from various sources.
  3. Upload your resume (PDF format).
  4. AI compares your resume with job descriptions, generating match scores.
  5. View top job matches, including score and reason for recommendation.
